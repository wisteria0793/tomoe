FAQ.

Q. Do you have a parking premises?

A. Yes, 3 cars can be parked in No.1 and No.2, 2 cars in No.3, and 1 car in No.4.

Q. What time is check-in and check-out?

A. Check-in is from 3:00 PM, and check-out is at 11:00 AM. For early check-in, please inquire and consult with us.

Q. Is it possible to stay without a reservation?

A. Yes, you can stay without a reservation on the same day. Please contact us on phone.

Q. Is smoking allowed?

A. All accommodations are non-smoking.

Q. What amenities are available?

A. Toothbrush/face towel/bath towel/shampoo/conditioner/body soap are provided in the bathroom. Pajamas are not provided, so please bring your own. Guesthouses are equipped with cooking facilities and tableware, so it is possible to prepare your own meals, but please check with us in advance if you need to prepare your own meals.
